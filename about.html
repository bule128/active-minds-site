About Us

Who we are and why we exist

Our Organisation

Active Minds Society is a youth-led non-profit organisation based in Durban, committed to empowering youth and children in underserved township communities through transformative education and sports initiatives.

Since our inception in 2019, we’ve been driven by the belief that every child, regardless of their socio-economic background, deserves access to quality learning experiences and opportunities to unlock their potential.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To inspire and educate by developing innovative and engaging learning experiences, blending education and sports to empower youth and communities.

Vision: To build a future where youth and communities thrive through innovative education and active participation.

Our Values

  • Innovation: We create forward-thinking programmes that address real needs.
  • Community: We foster collaboration, mentorship, and support networks.
  • Inclusivity: We ensure access to opportunities for everyone, regardless of background.

Our Objectives

  • Expand participation in sports and physical activity by 45% within 5 years among youth aged 4–25.
  • Implement innovative learning methods across 18 schools to foster leadership and teamwork.
  • Host 4 annual health and wellness events targeting 100+ participants per event.

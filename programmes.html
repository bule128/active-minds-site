Our Programmes

Building stronger communities through education, sports, and health

Active Swimmers Club

Launched in December 2023, the Active Swimmers Club introduces children and youth in underserved communities to swimming. We offer beginner and intermediate lessons for ages 4–18, with plans for competitive training. The programme promotes water safety, builds confidence, and encourages a healthy lifestyle.

Fundiseka "Find Your Focus" Workshops

These workshops prepare high school learners for life beyond school. Topics include financial literacy, entrepreneurship, digital literacy, career guidance, and soft skills like teamwork and problem-solving. Our goal is to equip learners with practical tools for informed decision-making and future success.

Community Fitness Project

The Community Fitness Project promotes physical and mental well-being for all ages. Activities include aerobics, strength training, and flexibility exercises, along with an annual Fitness Fun Day. Partnering with local healthcare providers, we also offer resources to enhance wellness and build stronger community ties.
